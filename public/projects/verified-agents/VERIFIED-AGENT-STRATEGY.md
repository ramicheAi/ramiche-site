## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Parallax is positioned to create the world's first **Verified AI Agent Marketplace** — a certification authority for AI professional services. The business model: interview domain experts, extract their knowledge into specialized AI agents, certify those agents through a rigorous verification process, and rent them at $100-$500/hr.

**Why this wins:** Anyone can build a chatbot. Nobody else has a verified, expert-trained, insured, certified agent marketplace backed by 20 battle-tested agents with 6+ months of operational infrastructure.

**The analogy:** This is domain squatting for AI. First to certify owns the category.

**First move:** Legal vertical (THEMIS) launches via the Atlanta lawyer meeting on Wednesday, March 12. Partnership with Eric. Revenue target: $540K Year 1, $3M Year 2, $12M Year 3.

**Three moats:** (1) The certification mark, (2) Expert relationships, (3) The platform (ClawHub + 20 agents + Relay SDK).

## PART 3: CERTIFICATION FRAMEWORK

### Certification Levels

| Level | Requirements | Use Cases | Oversight |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-----------|
| **Bronze** | AI-generated knowledge base, no expert interview | Basic tasks, information retrieval | Full disclaimer, limited scope |
| **Silver** | Single expert interview (4-8 hours), 100-scenario test, 80%+ pass rate | Mid-complexity tasks, structured outputs | Disclaimer + periodic audit |
| **Gold** | Multiple expert interviews (20+ hours), 100-scenario test, 90%+ pass rate | Full professional-grade output | Advisory board review, quarterly audit |
| **Platinum** | Ongoing expert supervision, continuous learning, annual re-cert | Enterprise-grade, high-stakes decisions | Named supervisor, real-time review triggers |

### Verification Process (Per Agent)

1. **Expert Identification** — Find the #1 domain expert (15+ years hands-on)
2. **Knowledge Extraction Interview** — 3-4 hours, recorded, structured (same framework as the 64-question attorney doc)
3. **Reasoning Framework Encoding** — Capture HOW they think, not just what they know (IRAC for law, diagnostic trees for medicine, design principles for creative)
4. **Test Battery Creation** — 100 domain-specific scenarios graded by the expert
5. **Certification Exam** — Agent must pass 90%+ to achieve Gold certification
6. **Advisory Board Validation** — 3-5 experts per vertical validate quality
7. **Annual Re-Certification** — Updated knowledge, new scenarios, quality audit
8. **Client Feedback Loop** — Rating system, quality audits, revocation if quality drops

### Certification Mark

- **"Parallax Verified"** — trademarked quality mark
- Displayed on agent profile, rental page, and all outputs
- Revocable if quality drops below threshold
- Trademark filing recommended (Class 42 — technology services)

## PART 5: LEGAL & REGULATORY LANDSCAPE

### Critical Legal Risks

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| **Unauthorized Practice of Law (UPL)** | CRITICAL | Position as "legal technology platform," not lawyer. Mandatory disclaimers. Attorney oversight board. UPL-compliant service boundaries. |
| **Fee-splitting (ABA Rule 5.4)** | HIGH | Structure as technology licensing fee, not legal fee split. Get specific guidance Wednesday. |
| **FL Bar Rule 4-5.5** | HIGH | Ensure THEMIS services stay within FL "legal information" vs "legal advice" boundaries. |
| **GA Rule 5.5** | HIGH | Same for Georgia — get state-specific boundaries from Atlanta attorney. |
| **Professional liability/malpractice** | HIGH | E&O insurance, limitation of liability in ToS, corporate shield (LLC or C-Corp), mandatory disclaimers. |
| **Data privacy (GDPR, CCPA)** | MEDIUM | Data handling policies, retention limits, client consent frameworks, no training on client data without permission. |
| **EU AI Act (high-risk classification)** | MEDIUM | Legal AI classified as high-risk. Compliance required for international expansion. Phase 3+ concern. |
| **Attorney-client privilege** | MEDIUM | Does NOT apply to AI-client communications. Build confidentiality framework independent of privilege. |

### Safe Harbor Opportunities

- **Arizona/Utah/California sandbox programs** — non-lawyer legal services allowed under regulatory sandboxes. Potential launchpad.
- **Document automation** — clearly outside UPL in most states
- **Legal research summaries** — generally safe
- **Compliance checklists** — generally safe
- **Contract templates with disclaimers** — gray area, state-dependent

### Key Questions for Wednesday (Priority Order)

1. Where is the exact line between "legal information" and "legal advice" in FL and GA?
2. Does our certification mark create MORE UPL liability by implying competence?
3. How do we structure payments to avoid fee-splitting violations?
4. What entity structure provides the best liability shield?
5. The malpractice walkthrough (Question 55) — trace the full liability chain
6. Can we get E&O coverage for AI legal services? What carriers?
