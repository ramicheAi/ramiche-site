## Follow-Up Cadence

Day 0: Initial outreach (DM or email)
Day 3: Follow up if no response ("Just bumping this — wanted to make sure you saw it")
Day 7: Value add ("Wrote a piece on [relevant topic] that might be useful → [blog link]")
Day 14: Final touch ("Last note — the free deployment offer expires [date]. No pressure.")

Then stop. No more than 4 touches. Respect > persistence.
