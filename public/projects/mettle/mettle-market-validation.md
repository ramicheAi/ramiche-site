## METTLE's Competitive Moat

### What competitors do (TeamUnify, TeamSnap, SwimTopia):
- **Administrative tools** — registration, billing, scheduling, communication
- No athlete development intelligence
- No gamification or motivation systems
- No AI-driven insights
- No parent engagement beyond email blasts

### What METTLE does differently:
- **Three-portal architecture** — Coach / Athlete / Parent (no competitor has all three as native experiences)
- **Gamified progression** — Rookie → Contender → Warrior → Elite → Captain → Legend
- **AI-powered athlete development** — not just times, but trends, predictions, and coaching recommendations
- **Meet management** — Hy-Tek parser, seed times, heat/lane, results, splits, export
- **PIN-based athlete access** — athletes access their own profile without email/password complexity
- **Biblical brand identity** — premium positioning (purple, scarlet, gold, blue) vs corporate gray

### The positioning line:
> "TeamUnify runs your office. METTLE transforms your athletes."
