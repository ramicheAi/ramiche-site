## 1. POINTS PROGRAM TERMS

### 1.1 Nature of Grit Points

Grit Points are promotional loyalty credits issued at METTLE's sole discretion to reward engagement with the METTLE platform (the "Platform"). Points:

(a) Have **no cash value**, monetary equivalent, or property value whatsoever, and shall not be construed as currency, stored value, electronic money, gift cards, gift certificates, prepaid access, or any other financial instrument or product under federal or state law, including but not limited to the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (15 U.S.C. 1693 et seq.), the Bank Secrecy Act (31 U.S.C. 5311 et seq.), state money transmitter statutes, or state unclaimed property laws;

(b) Represent only a **limited, revocable license** granted by METTLE to participate in the Program under these Points Terms, and do not create any vested right, entitlement, property interest, or chose in action in favor of any participant;

(c) Are **non-transferable** and may not be sold, bartered, traded, gifted, assigned, bequeathed, or otherwise conveyed to any other person or entity, whether for consideration or gratuitously. Any attempted transfer is void and may result in forfeiture of the transferred Points and termination from the Program;

(d) Are **non-exchangeable** for cash, check, wire transfer, cryptocurrency, or any other medium of exchange, and no request for cash redemption will be honored under any circumstances; and

(e) Are issued on a **per-account basis** and may not be pooled, combined, or aggregated across multiple METTLE accounts, teams, or organizations, except as expressly provided in Section 3 (Minor Participants).

### 1.2 Earning Points

Points may be earned exclusively through qualifying activities conducted within the METTLE Platform, including but not limited to:

(a) **Training Activities** — Attendance at scheduled practices, completion of assigned workout sets, and logging of training sessions as verified through the Platform;

(b) **Performance Milestones** — Achievement of personal bests, qualifying times, competition placements, or other performance benchmarks as configured by the participant's Coach Administrator ("Coach") within the Platform;

(c) **Platform Engagement** — Consistent use of the Platform, streak maintenance, goal completion, and other engagement activities as designated by METTLE or the Coach from time to time; and

(d) **Promotional Awards** — Bonus Points issued by METTLE or the Coach in connection with special promotions, challenges, or seasonal campaigns.

The specific Point values for qualifying activities are determined by METTLE in its sole discretion, subject to Coach-level configuration where the Platform permits. METTLE reserves the right to modify, adjust, or recalculate Point accrual rates at any time, with or without notice, provided that any material reduction in earning rates shall be subject to the notice provisions set forth in Section 5.1.

Points are credited upon verification of the qualifying activity by the Platform. METTLE is not responsible for Points not credited due to system errors, data synchronization failures, or incomplete activity records. Participants may dispute Point accrual errors by contacting their Coach or METTLE support within thirty (30) days of the alleged error.

### 1.3 Points Expiration

Points shall expire and be permanently forfeited upon the occurrence of any of the following:

(a) **Inactivity Expiration.** If a participant's account has no qualifying earning activity and no qualifying redemption activity for a continuous period of twelve (12) months (the "Inactivity Period"), all accumulated Points in that account shall expire automatically without further notice. For purposes of this provision, "qualifying activity" means any action that results in the earning or redemption of at least one (1) Point. Mere login to the Platform without earning or redeeming Points does not constitute qualifying activity.

(b) **Account Termination.** As set forth in Section 5.3.

(c) **Program Cancellation.** As set forth in Section 1.4.

METTLE shall make commercially reasonable efforts to notify participants via the email address associated with their account at least thirty (30) days prior to the expiration of Points under subsection (a), but failure to provide such notice shall not prevent the expiration of Points or create any liability on the part of METTLE.

### 1.4 Program Modification and Cancellation

METTLE reserves the right, in its sole and absolute discretion, to modify, suspend, or cancel the Program, in whole or in part, at any time, for any reason, subject to the notice provisions in Section 5.1. In the event of Program cancellation:

(a) METTLE shall provide at least thirty (30) days' notice to all active participants;

(b) Participants shall have a minimum of thirty (30) days from the date of cancellation notice to redeem any outstanding Points balance, subject to the redemption terms in Section 2; and

(c) Any Points remaining unredeemed at the conclusion of the redemption wind-down period shall be forfeited without compensation.

### 1.5 Anti-Fraud and Program Integrity

METTLE reserves the right to audit Point balances, void fraudulently or erroneously earned Points, and suspend or terminate any participant's access to the Program if METTLE determines, in its reasonable judgment, that the participant has engaged in fraud, abuse, manipulation of Platform data, exploitation of system errors, or any other conduct that undermines the integrity of the Program. Points voided under this Section are not subject to appeal or reinstatement.

## 2. REDEMPTION TERMS

### 2.1 Redemption Channels

Points may be redeemed exclusively through the following channels within the METTLE Platform:

(a) **Shop Discounts.** Points may be applied as a discount toward the purchase of eligible merchandise, apparel, equipment, or other goods offered through the METTLE integrated team shop (the "Shop"). Discounts are applied at checkout and reduce the total amount charged to the participant's (or, for minor participants, the parent/guardian's) payment method on file. Points applied to a Shop purchase are non-refundable once the order is confirmed, except as provided in Section 4.4.

(b) **Billing Credits.** Points may be applied as a credit against recurring Platform subscription fees, team dues, or other charges invoiced through the METTLE billing system (collectively, "Billing Charges"). Billing Credits are applied to the next eligible billing cycle following redemption and reduce the amount charged to the applicable payment method on file.

Points may not be redeemed for cash, external gift cards, third-party services, or any product or service outside the METTLE Platform.

### 2.2 Redemption Rates and Values

The redemption value of Points shall be established by METTLE and published on the Platform. As of the effective date of these Points Terms, the baseline redemption rate is set forth in the Platform's Points Program documentation. METTLE reserves the right to modify redemption rates at any time, subject to the notice provisions in Section 5.1, provided that:

(a) Any reduction in redemption value shall not apply retroactively to Points already earned prior to the effective date of such change; and

(b) Points earned after the effective date of a redemption rate change shall be redeemable at the new rate.

### 2.3 Minimum Redemption Thresholds

Redemptions are subject to the following minimum thresholds:

(a) **Shop Discounts.** A minimum Points balance as specified in the Platform's current Program documentation must be accumulated before Points may be redeemed toward a Shop purchase. Partial redemptions are permitted provided the minimum threshold is met.

(b) **Billing Credits.** A minimum Points balance as specified in the Platform's current Program documentation must be accumulated before Points may be applied as a Billing Credit. Partial redemptions are permitted provided the minimum threshold is met.

Minimum thresholds are established and may be adjusted by METTLE, and are published on the Platform. METTLE shall provide at least fifteen (15) days' notice before increasing any minimum redemption threshold.

### 2.4 Coach-Configurable Monthly Credit Cap

To ensure responsible use of Billing Credits and to preserve team financial sustainability, the Platform provides Coach Administrators with the ability to set a maximum monthly Billing Credit allowance per participant (the "Monthly Credit Cap"):

(a) The Monthly Credit Cap limits the aggregate dollar value of Billing Credits that a single participant may redeem within a calendar month;

(b) The Monthly Credit Cap is configured by the Coach at the team or organization level within the Platform's administrative settings;

(c) If no Monthly Credit Cap is set by the Coach, METTLE's system-wide default cap, as published on the Platform, shall apply;

(d) Points that a participant is unable to redeem in a given month due to the Monthly Credit Cap remain in the participant's account and may be redeemed in subsequent months, subject to the expiration provisions of Section 1.3;

(e) The Monthly Credit Cap applies solely to Billing Credits and does not restrict redemption toward Shop Discounts; and

(f) Coaches may adjust the Monthly Credit Cap at any time. Changes take effect at the beginning of the next calendar month following the change.

### 2.5 Redemption Processing

All redemptions are processed through the METTLE Platform and are subject to verification. METTLE reserves the right to delay, deny, or reverse any redemption that it reasonably believes is fraudulent, erroneous, or in violation of these Points Terms. Redemptions are final once processed, except as provided in Section 4.4 (Refund Handling).

## 3. MINOR PARTICIPANTS

### 3.1 Applicability

This Section applies to all Program participants who are under the age of eighteen (18) (or the age of majority in their jurisdiction of residence, if higher) ("Minor Participants"). METTLE's treatment of Minor Participants is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA," 15 U.S.C. 6501-6506) and applicable state laws governing minors' online activities and commercial transactions.

### 3.2 Parental/Guardian Account Requirement

(a) A Minor Participant may earn Points through qualifying activities on the Platform, but **all Point redemptions must be initiated, authorized, and completed through a linked parent or legal guardian account** (the "Parent Account"). Minor Participants cannot independently redeem Points, make purchases, or apply Billing Credits.

(b) Each Minor Participant's account must be linked to a verified Parent Account. The parent or legal guardian associated with the Parent Account (the "Authorized Parent") is solely responsible for all redemption decisions made with respect to the Minor Participant's Point balance.

(c) The Authorized Parent may view the Minor Participant's Point balance, earning history, and redemption history through the Parent Account dashboard at any time.

(d) The Authorized Parent may restrict, limit, or disable the Minor Participant's ability to earn or request redemption of Points at any time through the Parent Account settings.

### 3.3 Prohibition on Direct Transactions by Minors

(a) Minor Participants **shall not** directly: (i) redeem Points for Shop Discounts; (ii) apply Points as Billing Credits; (iii) complete any purchase or financial transaction through the Platform; or (iv) provide or authorize the use of any payment method.

(b) All financial transactions associated with a Minor Participant's account, including but not limited to Shop purchases and Billing Credit applications, must be executed by the Authorized Parent through the Parent Account.

(c) Minor Participants may submit redemption requests through the Platform, but such requests shall be treated as notifications to the Authorized Parent and shall have no binding effect until affirmatively approved and executed by the Authorized Parent.

### 3.4 Verifiable Parental Consent for Performance Data Linkage

(a) Because the Program links athletic performance data (including training attendance, workout completion, competitive results, and derived metrics) to purchasing and billing activity, METTLE requires **verifiable parental consent** ("VPC") before enabling Program participation for any Minor Participant.

(b) VPC shall be obtained through one of the following methods, as permitted under COPPA and its implementing regulations (16 C.F.R. Part 312):

   (i) Signed consent form submitted electronically via the Platform using the Authorized Parent's verified account credentials, combined with a confirmation email sent to the Authorized Parent's verified email address;

   (ii) A credit card, debit card, or other online payment system transaction that provides notification of each discrete transaction to the primary account holder, where such system is used to charge a nominal verification fee (which shall be fully refunded);

   (iii) A toll-free telephone number staffed by trained personnel; or

   (iv) Such other method as may be approved by the Federal Trade Commission under 16 C.F.R. 312.5(b).

(c) The VPC must specifically disclose and obtain consent for:

   (i) The categories of performance data that will be linked to purchasing and billing activity;

   (ii) The purposes for which such linkage is used (i.e., determining Point earning and enabling redemption);

   (iii) The Authorized Parent's right to review, modify, or delete the Minor Participant's performance data, and to revoke consent and terminate Program participation at any time; and

   (iv) METTLE's data retention and deletion practices with respect to Minor Participants' performance data.

(d) The Authorized Parent may revoke consent at any time by contacting METTLE at the address provided in the Platform or by adjusting settings in the Parent Account. Upon revocation, the Minor Participant's Program participation shall be suspended, and unredeemed Points shall be retained for a period of ninety (90) days (during which the Authorized Parent may re-consent), after which they shall be forfeited.

### 3.5 Age Verification

METTLE relies on the account registration information provided by the Coach and/or Authorized Parent to determine whether a participant is a Minor Participant. METTLE reserves the right to request additional documentation to verify age or parental/guardian status at any time.

## 5. MODIFICATION AND TERMINATION

### 5.1 Modification of Points Terms

(a) METTLE reserves the right to modify these Points Terms at any time. Modifications are categorized as follows:

   (i) **Material Changes.** Any modification that materially affects participants' earning rates, redemption values, expiration policies, or forfeiture conditions constitutes a "Material Change." METTLE shall provide at least **thirty (30) days' advance written notice** of Material Changes via (A) email to the address associated with the participant's account (or the Authorized Parent's account, for Minor Participants), and (B) prominent notice on the Platform. Material Changes take effect on the date specified in the notice, which shall be no fewer than thirty (30) days from the date of notice.

   (ii) **Non-Material Changes.** Modifications that do not materially affect participants' substantive rights under the Program (e.g., formatting changes, clarifications, addition of new earning opportunities) may be made at any time and take effect upon posting to the Platform. METTLE shall make reasonable efforts to notify participants of non-material changes but is not required to provide advance notice.

(b) Continued participation in the Program after the effective date of any modification constitutes acceptance of the modified Points Terms. Participants who do not agree to modified Points Terms must cease participation in the Program and may redeem any outstanding Points balance in accordance with Section 2 prior to the effective date of the modification.

### 5.2 Voluntary Termination by Participant

(a) A participant (or Authorized Parent, for Minor Participants) may terminate participation in the Program at any time by contacting METTLE support or adjusting account settings in the Platform.

(b) Upon voluntary termination, the participant shall have thirty (30) days to redeem any outstanding Points balance. Points remaining unredeemed at the conclusion of the thirty-day period shall be forfeited without compensation.

(c) Voluntary termination of Program participation does not affect the participant's METTLE account or subscription status.

### 5.3 Termination for Cause by METTLE

(a) METTLE may terminate a participant's access to the Program immediately and without prior notice if METTLE determines, in its reasonable judgment, that the participant has:

   (i) Violated these Points Terms or the METTLE Master Terms of Service;

   (ii) Engaged in fraud, misrepresentation, or manipulation of Platform data or Program mechanics;

   (iii) Attempted to transfer, sell, or otherwise convey Points in violation of Section 1.1(c);

   (iv) Engaged in abusive, threatening, or harassing conduct toward METTLE personnel, Coaches, or other participants; or

   (v) Otherwise acted in a manner that METTLE reasonably determines is detrimental to the Program or the Platform.

(b) **Upon termination for cause, all Points in the terminated participant's account shall be immediately and permanently forfeited without compensation, reinstatement, or appeal.** METTLE shall have no obligation to provide notice prior to forfeiture under this Section.

(c) Termination from the Program for cause may, at METTLE's discretion, result in concurrent termination of the participant's METTLE account and subscription, subject to the refund provisions of Section 5.4.

### 5.4 Refunds for Prepaid Subscriptions upon Termination

(a) If a Coach's or participant's METTLE subscription is terminated by METTLE (whether in connection with Program termination or otherwise), and the Coach or participant has prepaid subscription fees for a period extending beyond the termination date, METTLE shall issue a **pro-rata refund** of the unused portion of the prepaid subscription fees, calculated on a daily basis from the effective date of termination through the end of the prepaid period.

(b) The pro-rata refund shall be calculated as follows:

   **Refund Amount = (Prepaid Fee / Total Days in Prepaid Period) x Remaining Days after Termination**

(c) The refund shall be issued to the original payment method within thirty (30) days of the termination date, less any applicable Stripe processing fees that are non-recoverable (as described in Section 4.4).

(d) Platform service fees attributable to the unused portion of the prepaid period shall be refunded in full to the Coach.

(e) **Exception:** No refund shall be issued if termination results from the participant's or Coach's material breach of these Points Terms or the METTLE Master Terms of Service, including but not limited to fraud, abuse, or violation of law. In such cases, all prepaid fees are forfeited as liquidated damages, not as a penalty, which the parties agree is a reasonable estimate of METTLE's damages, given the difficulty of calculating actual damages from such breaches.

### 5.5 Survival

Sections 1.1 (Nature of Grit Points), 4 (Liability and Disclaimers), 5.3(b) (Forfeiture upon Termination for Cause), and 5.5 (Survival), together with any other provisions that by their nature are intended to survive, shall survive the termination or expiration of these Points Terms or a participant's participation in the Program.
