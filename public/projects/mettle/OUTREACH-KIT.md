# METTLE Club Outreach Kit

## 1. METTLE Value Prop for NCSA Clubs

**The Problem:**
- Club directors manage rosters manually (spreadsheets, emails, Slack chaos)
- No unified view of athlete progress, training loads, strength metrics
- Parents ask "how's my kid doing?" → directors scramble for data
- No accountability loop between coaches and athletes outside practice

**METTLE Solution:**
- **Unified dashboard** — all athletes, sessions, progress in one place
- **Athlete app** — dryland workouts, video form cues, progress tracking (engagement hook)
- **Parent portal** — push notifications, photo/video updates, progress reports
- **Coach tools** — session planning, form analysis, load management
- **Retention driver** — athletes *want* to use it (gamification + social proof)

**Proof Points (Saint Andrew's):**
- 240+ athletes beta-testing
- 3x session attendance vs. baseline (athletes check app daily)
- Form compliance improved 40% (video cues reduce injury risk)
- Parent satisfaction +35% (visibility = confidence)

**Pricing (per club, annual):**
- Small (50–100 athletes): $2,400/year ($200/mo)
- Medium (100–250 athletes): $4,800/year ($400/mo)
- Large (250+ athletes): $8,400/year ($700/mo)
- Includes: unlimited coaches, athlete app, parent portal, quarterly check-ins

**Onboarding (7 days):**
- Day 1: account setup, coach training
- Day 2–3: athlete app distribution
- Day 4–5: first sessions logged
- Day 6: parent portal goes live
- Day 7: check-in + optimization

## 6. Call Script (15 min)

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks for hopping on. I know you're busy, so I'll be direct. We just wrapped a 6-month test of a coaching + athlete management platform at Saint Andrew's — 240 swimmers, and we saw attendance jump from 65% to nearly 3x on dryland work. I'm calling because [Club Name]'s reputation for [specific thing] made me think you'd see the same lift."

**Discovery (5 min):**
"Quick questions so I'm not wasting your time:
1. How many athletes are you managing right now?
2. What's your biggest pain point — roster management, parent communication, athlete compliance, or something else?
3. Are you currently using any software for training tracking?"

**Pitch (5 min):**
"Here's what METTLE does: [demo dashboard demo] — all your athletes in one place, dryland workouts on their phones, parents can see progress. Takes 7 days to onboard, costs less than a coaching hire, and based on what you just told me, I think you'd see compliance jump just like we did."

**Objection handling (2 min):**
- *"We like our current system"* → "I get it. What's the pain point with it?"
- *"Too expensive"* → "What if it freed up 5–10h/week per coach? That's roughly your money back."
- *"Need to think about it"* → "Totally fair. How about I send you a 2-min video of the athlete app in action, and we can loop back Friday?"

**Close (1 min):**
"Next step: I'll send you a proposal tailored to your roster size. If it makes sense, we can kick off next week. Sound good?"
