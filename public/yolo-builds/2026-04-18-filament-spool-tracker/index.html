Material Quick Reference
PLA
Easiest to print. Biodegradable. Low warp. Brittle under stress. Not heat-resistant (softens ~55°C).
Nozzle: 190-220°C • Bed: 50-65°C • Enclosure: No
PETG
Strong, flexible, chemical-resistant. Good layer adhesion. Strings more than PLA. Heat-resistant to ~80°C.
Nozzle: 220-250°C • Bed: 70-85°C • Enclosure: Optional
ABS
Strong, heat-resistant (~100°C). Warps heavily — enclosure required. Fumes — ventilate. Acetone-smoothable.
Nozzle: 230-260°C • Bed: 90-110°C • Enclosure: Required
TPU
Flexible, rubber-like. Great for grips, cases, seals. Slow print speeds required. Direct drive extruder preferred.
Nozzle: 210-230°C • Bed: 40-60°C • Enclosure: No
ASA
UV-resistant ABS alternative. Outdoor-ready. Less warp than ABS but still needs enclosure. Tough and durable.
Nozzle: 240-260°C • Bed: 90-110°C • Enclosure: Required
Nylon
Extremely strong, wear-resistant. Absorbs moisture — dry before use. High warp. Excellent for functional parts.
Nozzle: 240-270°C • Bed: 70-90°C • Enclosure: Required
