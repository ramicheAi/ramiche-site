HOW IT WORKS

Click anywhere on the build plate to drop the selected shape. Objects fall under simulated gravity, collide with each other, and settle on the bed surface. Use this to experiment with part arrangement — the same physics that govern real print bed packing.

WHY PACKING MATTERS

A Bambu A1 build plate is 256 × 256 mm. Every print job has fixed overhead — heating, leveling, purge line. Fitting more parts per batch = fewer jobs = less overhead = lower cost per part. But pack too tight and you get adhesion interference, thermal warping, and purge contamination. The sweet spot is 60-75% utilization.
