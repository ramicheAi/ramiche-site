How Conversion Works

USA Swimming uses empirically-derived conversion factors based on thousands of elite performances. Factors account for:

  • Turn differential — more turns in short course = faster times due to underwater speed off walls
  • Stroke mechanics — some strokes benefit more from walls than others
  • Distance scaling — longer events have more turns, larger differentials
  • Gender differences — slight variations in conversion factors between male and female swimmers
Note: Conversions are approximations. Actual performance varies based on an athlete's individual strengths (turns, underwaters, endurance).
