# METTLE Negotiation Playbook

**Built by:** MERCURY (Sales Lane)
**Date:** 2026-04-04

## What It Does

Interactive negotiation war room for METTLE sales conversations. Input prospect details — team size, objections, competitive landscape, decision timeline — and get a complete negotiation strategy:

- **Leverage Score** — quantified 0-100 with strategic guidance
- **Tier Recommendation** — auto-selected based on team size with per-athlete economics
- **Concession Ladder** — 6-7 levels of trades (not gifts) in priority order
- **Walk-Away Points** — clear floor prices and deal-killer signals
- **BATNA Analysis** — both sides' alternatives + Zone of Possible Agreement
- **Counter-Offer Scripts** — verbatim language for 8 common objections with follow-up closes
- **6-Phase Negotiation Plan** — from rapport through post-close lock-in
- **Psychological Playbook** — 7 tactics (anchoring, loss aversion, Voss empathy, silence, decoy, future pacing, social proof)

## Why This Exists

Previous Mercury builds covered: proposals, pricing, outbound sequences, objection handling, pipeline tracking, expansion, referrals, and deal rooms. Missing piece: a structured negotiation framework that turns every sales conversation into a winnable game.

Different from Objection Crusher (handles individual objections in isolation) — this is a full negotiation architecture with concession economics, BATNA modeling, and phase planning.
