# METTLE Deal Room

**Built by:** MERCURY
**Date:** 2026-04-14
**Category:** Sales & Revenue

## What It Is

A live deal prep war room — the single screen a salesperson opens before every sales call. Prospect intel, objection playbooks, competitor kill sheets, pricing calculator, and a real-time closing checklist in one view.

## Features

- **Prospect Profile Builder:** Enter org name, contact, athlete count, current solution — the room configures itself around the deal context.
- **Objection Handler Matrix:** 12 pre-loaded objections with scripted responses, calibrated labels (concern vs. stall vs. smokescreen), and tactical notes from Mercury's playbook.
- **Competitor Comparison Engine:** Side-by-side feature comparison against TeamUnify, SwimTopia, TeamSnap. Auto-highlights METTLE advantages based on the prospect's stated pain points.
- **Live Pricing Calculator:** Tier selector with annual/monthly toggle, multi-team discount modeling, ROI projector showing cost-per-athlete and projected retention lift.
- **Closing Checklist:** 8-step call flow with checkboxes — from rapport building through trial close to commitment ask. Each step has a micro-script.
- **Deal Score Gauge:** Real-time deal health score (0-100) computed from: budget confirmed, timeline exists, decision-maker identified, pain articulated, competition known.
- **Notes & Follow-up Panel:** Capture live notes during the call with auto-timestamping. Generate follow-up email template on one click.
- **Dark Mode UI:** Designed for second-monitor use during live calls — low glare, high contrast, scannable.
- **LocalStorage persistence:** Deal prep sessions survive refresh.

## Sales Philosophy Embedded

- "The best close happens before the pitch" — the prep IS the weapon.
- Objection scripts use tactical empathy (Voss) + reframing (Pink) + urgency architecture (Belfort).
- No discount buttons anywhere. Only value-add options.
- Competitor section never trash-talks — positions METTLE as complementary or superior on outcomes, never on feature count.

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any browser. Create a deal room, prep your call, crush it.
