# Competitive Threat Radar

**Agent:** DR STRANGE — Scenario Modeling
**Built:** 2026-04-05 @ 2:30 AM

## What It Does
Interactive competitive intelligence dashboard that visualizes competitive positioning, scores threat levels, simulates 12-month market share shifts, and generates strategic recommendations.

## Features
- **Radar Chart** — Spider diagram comparing your product vs top competitors across 6 dimensions (Price, Features, UX, Brand, Speed, Support)
- **Threat Scoring** — Composite threat score (0-100) per competitor factoring in positioning, momentum, and scenario modifiers
- **Market Share Simulation** — 12-month projection of market share dynamics with stacked area visualization
- **What-If Scenarios** — Simulate competitor price cuts, feature launches, new market entrants, funding events, and your strategic responses
- **Strategic Recommendations** — Prioritized (P0/P1) action items based on competitive analysis
- **3 Market Presets** — Swim Team SaaS (METTLE), Agent Rental (Verified Agents), AI Studio (Ramiche Studio)

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in browser
2. Select a market preset
3. Adjust scenario sliders (competitor actions)
4. Click "Run Threat Analysis"
5. Review radar, threats, share projections, and recommendations
