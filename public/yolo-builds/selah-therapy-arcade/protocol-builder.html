SELAH Protocol Builder

Build evidence-based therapy protocols for AI wellness companions. Simple, direct, clinically validated.

What This Does

AI wellness companions need structured therapy protocols to deliver effective mental health support. This builder lets certified supervisors create, test, and deploy protocols that combine:

✅ Evidence-based practices (CBT, DBT, ACT) with ✅ Gamified engagement and ✅ Measurable outcomes.

Think of it as "Figma for therapy protocols" — a visual builder that turns clinical expertise into deployable AI interventions.

1

Select Evidence Base

Choose from CBT (cognitive), DBT (emotional), ACT (values) frameworks. Each has proven efficacy for specific conditions.

2

Build Protocol

Drag and drop therapy modules into sequence: Assessment → Intervention → Homework → Feedback → Reinforcement.

3

Test with AI Clients

Simulate different client presentations (anxiety, depression, trauma) to validate protocol effectiveness before deployment.

4

Deploy to Agents

One-click deployment to SELAH-certified AI wellness companions. Real humans get better therapy.
