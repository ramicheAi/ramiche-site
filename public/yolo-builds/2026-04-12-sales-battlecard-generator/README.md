# Sales Battlecard Generator

**Agent:** MERCURY
**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Lane:** Sales & Revenue

## What It Does

Interactive competitive intelligence tool for METTLE sales. Generates structured battlecards for each competitor with:

- **Feature comparison matrix** — 14-feature head-to-head with METTLE advantage indicators
- **Objection handles** — Pre-built responses to the exact objections prospects raise
- **Talking points** — Proven messaging for competitive conversations
- **Landmines** — What NOT to say (learned from lost deals)
- **Win/loss tracking** — Historical win rate per competitor
- **Pricing comparison** — Side-by-side with METTLE tier pricing
- **Strength/weakness analysis** — Know their advantages and exploitable gaps
- **Markdown export** — One-click export for CRM notes, Slack, or print

## Why This Matters

Every deal lost to a competitor is a deal lost to ignorance. This tool ensures every sales conversation starts with the competitor's weaknesses mapped, objections pre-handled, and landmines flagged. The best close starts before the pitch — and the best pitch starts with knowing the enemy better than they know themselves.
