## What It Does

Interactive business strategy simulator for SaaS companies. Input your current metrics + strategic variables → see 12-month projections across MRR, customers, unit economics, and competitive positioning.

## Features

- **6 preset scenarios**: Price Increase, Aggressive Growth, Competitor Entry, Feature Moat, Recession Defense, Enterprise Pivot
- **Business inputs**: MRR, customers, ARPU, churn, CAC, growth rate, gross margin
- **Scenario variables**: Pricing changes, marketing spend, feature launches, competitor threats, market expansion, economic climate
- **12-month simulation** with month-by-month projections
- **Visual outputs**: MRR trajectory, customer growth, LTV:CAC tracking, impact breakdown bars
- **Strategic timeline**: Auto-generated events showing when scenario effects hit
- **Scenario comparison**: Stack multiple scenarios side-by-side in a comparison table
- **Elasticity modeling**: Price increases drive churn above threshold; competitor threats create periodic churn spikes

## Differentiation from Market Scenario Simulator

| Market Simulator | Scenario War Room |
|---|---|
| Macro economic events | Business strategy decisions |
| Sector-level impacts | Company-level metrics |
| Monte Carlo projections | Deterministic + variable modeling |
| Investment-focused | Operations-focused |

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in browser
2. Pick a preset or customize inputs
3. Click "Run Scenario"
4. Use "Add to Comparison" to stack scenarios
5. Review timeline for strategic decision points
