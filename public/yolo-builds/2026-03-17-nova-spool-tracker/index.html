04 — FILAMENT TIPS

Storage Best Practices

PLA tolerates ambient humidity for weeks. PETG absorbs slowly — reseal after use. Nylon & PVA are extremely hygroscopic — store in vacuum bags with desiccant at all times. TPU absorbs moisture that causes bubbling and popping during extrusion. Rule of thumb: if filament snaps instead of bending, it's wet. Dry at 50-70°C for 4-8 hours.

When to Reorder

A typical benchy uses ~15g. A phone case uses ~30-50g. A functional enclosure can burn 200-400g. Keep at least one backup spool of your primary material. Running out mid-print wastes the entire job — the A1 cannot resume with a new spool color-matched.
