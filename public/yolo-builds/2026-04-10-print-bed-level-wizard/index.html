NOVA Print Bed Level Wizard

Interactive bed leveling guide with mesh visualization

Before You Start

This wizard guides you through manual bed leveling with real-time mesh visualization. Works with any FDM printer — Bambu Lab, Creality, Prusa, or custom builds.


What You'll Need

• A sheet of standard printer paper (0.1mm thickness)
• Access to your printer's corner adjustment knobs
• 5-10 minutes of patience


How It Works

You'll measure the nozzle-to-bed gap at 5 points (4 corners + center). The wizard calculates tilt, generates a mesh deviation map, and tells you exactly which knobs to turn and by how much.

Measure Corner Heights

Slide the paper under the nozzle at each point. Use the slider to record how tight the paper feels — from loose (gap too big) to tight (nozzle too close).
