## The call script (≤45 sec — the goal is a 10-min look, NOT a close on the phone)

**OPEN (disarm + permission):**
> "Hi, is the owner or manager around? — Hey, I'll be real with you, this is a cold call, give me 20 seconds and hang up on me if it's useless, fair? … I'm Ramon, I'm local. I went to look you up online before calling and I couldn't find a website — and when I asked ChatGPT for a [good Colombian spot / barbershop] in [Miami], you didn't come up but a few of your competitors did."

**LABEL THE GAP (the moment):**
> "So basically anybody who's new to the area and searches their phone or asks AI 'where should I eat tonight' — they're getting sent to the other guys, not you. You're invisible to everyone who isn't already a regular."

**COST OF INACTION + DREAM (3-pillar):**
> "I build the thing that fixes that — a clean site that Google AND ChatGPT actually read and recommend, online ordering/booking built in, live in days. The last spot I did this for started getting found by people who'd never heard of them. That's new tables every week you're leaving on the table right now."

**THE ASK (BAMFAM, tiny next step — not the sale):**
> "I don't want to sell you anything on the phone. Let me build you a free sample of what your homepage could look like and text it to you — if you like it we talk, if not no hard feelings. What's the best number to text? And are you around tomorrow 2pm or Thursday morning for 10 minutes to look at it together?"

**OBJECTIONS (AAA — Acknowledge, Associate, Ask):**
- *"I'm too busy / it's lunch rush"* → "That's exactly why I'll keep it to 10 min — when's your slow hour, 3pm?"
- *"I have a guy / my nephew does it"* → "Love that — does it show up when you ask ChatGPT for [your food]? If yes you're set. If not, let me just text you the sample, free, and you compare."
- *"I don't need a website, I'm on Instagram"* → "Instagram's great for regulars. The site is for the stranger Googling at 7pm who's never heard of you — and Google/AI can't recommend an Instagram the way they recommend a real site."
- *"How much?"* → "Builds start around $1,500 and I'm doing a few at a founder rate for case studies — but honestly let's see if you even like the sample first. No point talking price on something you haven't seen."

**Reinforce:** get the cell + the time → text the sample same day → confirm the 10-min look = **booked meeting.**
