# METTLE — Coach Quick-Start Guide

## Getting Started (5 minutes)

### 1. Open the App
Go to your team's METTLE URL in any browser (works on phone, tablet, or computer).

### 2. Log In
- Enter the **Master PIN** provided by your program administrator
- First time? Click **Register** to create your coach account (name + password)
- Already registered? Enter your name and password to log in

### 3. Import Your Roster
- Tap the **Import** button (top of the roster panel)
- Paste or upload a CSV with columns: **Name, Age, Gender, Group**
  - Only Name is required — Age, Gender, and Group are optional
  - Group names auto-match to your team's groups (e.g., "Platinum", "Gold", "Silver")
- Athletes appear instantly in the roster

### 4. Check-In Athletes at Practice
- Select the **group** from the dropdown
- Choose session mode: **Pool**, **Weight**, or **Meet**
- Tap an athlete's name to mark them **Present**
- Tap checkpoints (effort, technique, etc.) to award XP
- Session auto-detects AM/PM from your schedule

## Key Features

### Roster Management
- **Add athletes** individually with the + button
- **Import/Export** CSV for bulk operations
- **7 groups** pre-configured (Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze 1, Bronze 2, Diving, Water Polo)
- Edit athlete details by tapping their name in the roster

### Gamification (XP & Levels)
Athletes earn XP for showing up and hitting checkpoints:
- **Levels:** Rookie → Contender → Warrior → Elite → Captain → Legend
- **Streaks:** Consecutive practice days multiply XP (3d = 1.25x, 7d = 1.5x, 30d = 2.0x)
- **Daily XP Cap:** Prevents runaway point inflation
- Athletes see their progress in the Athlete Portal

### Quests
- Create challenges for athletes (e.g., "Attend 5 practices this week")
- Athletes submit proof → you **Approve** or **Deny**
- Look for the pulsing badge icon — it means pending reviews

### Schedule
- View/edit weekly practice schedule
- Add Pool, Weight, or Dryland sessions
- Times, locations, and session types are all configurable

### Analytics
- Attendance trends (30-day rolling)
- Average XP per day
- Longest streaks
- Group-level breakdowns

### Team Challenges
- Set team-wide goals (e.g., "90% attendance this week")
- Progress bar visible to all athletes
- Completed challenges award bonus XP

### Parent Portal
Parents log in with their own PIN and see:
- Their child's XP, level, and streak
- Growth trends and badges
- Encouragement tools
- COPPA-compliant — no direct messaging

## Tips
- **End sessions** when practice is done — tap "End Session" or it auto-resets after 90 minutes of inactivity
- **Export regularly** — CSV export gives you a backup of your full roster + stats
- The app works offline (data saved locally) and syncs to the cloud when connected
- Everything saves automatically — no "Save" button needed
